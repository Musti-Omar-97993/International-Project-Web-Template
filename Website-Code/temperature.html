Temperature Control in Hydroponics

Temperature is a critical factor in hydroponic systems. Proper temperature regulation ensures optimal plant growth and prevents issues like root rot and poor nutrient absorption.

Ideal Temperature Range

Most plants thrive in temperatures between 18°C and 25°C. Deviations can lead to stress and affect plant health.

Methods to Maintain Temperature

  • Using water chillers or heaters to regulate nutrient solution temperature.
  • Providing proper ventilation to avoid heat buildup.
  • Using grow lights that emit minimal heat.
