Air Circulation in Hydroponics

Proper air circulation is essential in hydroponic systems to provide plants with sufficient oxygen and prevent diseases.

Importance of Air Movement

Good airflow helps in strengthening plant stems, preventing mold, and maintaining a balanced humidity level.

Ways to Improve Air Circulation

  • Using oscillating fans to keep air moving.
  • Installing an exhaust system to remove stale air.
  • Ensuring proper spacing between plants.
